Relating to us

Born from a love story, driven by a commitment to natural healing. Our products began with a simple need for truly effective lip care, leading us to craft potent balms and beyond with rare ingredients sourced from extreme environments. These “skin health warriors” are designed for deep healing and are now part of our exclusive bundles of well-being. We cultivate and source nutrient-dense ingredients, prioritizing high medicinal value.

Step into a world where Ayurveda’s most sacred rituals are refined into exquisite, high-performance elixirs. At Peshagi, we craft nature’s most potent remedies into a luxurious self-healing experience.

The word “Peshagi” means an offering, something given in advance for a new beginning. Our philosophy is rooted in this essence, delivering high-potency, 100% natural formulations designed to honor your skin and soul.

Peshagi was not born from trends, but from necessity. When synthetic formulations failed, and even the finest “organic” alternatives did not meet our standards, we turned to nature’s rarest and most potent elements, sourcing from extreme, nutrient-rich environments to create our first elixir.

What started as a simple blend, a lip balm crafted in a humble kitchen, soon became the foundation of something greater: a brand committed to high-value, deeply restorative botanical remedies for the discerning / luxury seeking connoisseurs.

At Peshagi, we serve a deeper need, the pursuit of purity, efficacy, and time-honored healing. Every ingredient is carefully cultivated or sourced at peak potency, ensuring that our formulations embody not just luxury, but profound medicinal integrity.
